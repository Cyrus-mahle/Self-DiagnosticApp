About

This self-diagnostic application is a tool that can help you to learn more about your symptoms and their potential causes. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, but it can be a helpful resource for understanding your health better and making informed decisions about your care. To use the application, simply enter your symptoms and answer a few questions about your medical history and lifestyle. The application will then provide you with a list of possible causes, along with recommendations for what to do next. It is important to note that the application is not perfect. It cannot diagnose medical conditions definitively, and it should not be used to replace a visit to your doctor. However, it can be a helpful tool for learning more about your symptoms and getting started on the path to better health. Here are some examples of how you can use the application: * If you are experiencing new or unusual symptoms, you can use the application to learn more about what they might mean. * If you are unsure whether or not you should see a doctor for your symptoms, the application can help you to make a decision. * If you are already seeing a doctor for a medical condition, you can use the application to learn more about your condition and its treatment options. If you have any questions or concerns about your symptoms, please do not hesitate to contact your doctor. **Disclaimer** This self-diagnostic application is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
